Gear

I've been an Ibanez endorser since 2015 and have been playing their guitars exclusively ever since. In 2018 my first signature electric guitar, the Ibanez MM1 hit the market and it's all I ever dreamed it would be.

The sounds, the looks, the versatility, the durabilty and last but not the least the inspiration it provides me is second to none. I've never felt more connected to an instrument in my entire life. I hope you are going to go to your local guitar dealer and give the Ibanez MM1 a test drive and if you do, be sure to let me know what you think of it!

The guitar player in me has never been so proud. But I'm also thankful for the support of so many great business partners and friends, without whom this project would have never seen the light of the day. Thank you Hiroki Rocky Oda, Kashiuchi Motoaki Mike Orrigo, Daniel Gäthke, Stephan Killermann and everyone at Ibanez Guitars and Ibanez Germany, I love you guys!!

Look forward to a HUGE amount of videos in the near future and many songs, albums, recordings, livestreams, lessons, videos, etc. that will be created using this beautiful instrument.

Strings, Services & Cables

IQS Strings

IQS Strings are my strings of choice. Wonderful tone right out of the box, very reliable and smooth as silk to play. I use their uncoated, nickel plated 10-46 gauge sets and at a 56 for 7-string work.

Risiro Guitar Service

Christian Ritz at Risiro Guitar Leipzig is responsible for my day to day guitar care. He does all my setups, repairs and modification in an extremely reliable manner. If you're around Leipzig area and need guitar work done, he is your man.
